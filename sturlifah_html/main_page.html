WHO IS HE

Hassan Al-khalifah

I have a large background in the oil and gas industries. Industries like this have given me a huge perspective on what real true hard work and teamwork can do when trying to carry out a huge feat. The experience and knowledge I have gained in such a field has ever changed me as a person. In more recent years I been involved in the procurement business. Exporting of U.S. goods has been a huge factor in my involvement. The success of international relations, sales and marketing have been huge stepping-stones to my success in the industry. Such a field has shaped and molded me on a whole new scale of professionalism in my life. And I owe it to this path for my now steady, sure bow as I aim and shoot for goals in my life. From swinging sledge hammers on rocky boats during stormy nights to closing extensive sales deals in the cool A/C of an office, I have not only gained physical, but mental endurance and strength as well. Such experience has made me who I am today. But despite the diversity of my career path, my true passion though has always been with my creativity. Anything from art to designing from disassembling and recreating, I have always found joy and great success when I can allow my creativity to immerse in my actions and passions. Not too long ago, I got inspired by the IT field, particularly mobile app and website designing. The idea that I could finally have the chance to use my creativity at its most by designing and creating functional yet creative applications and websites overwhelmingly peaked my interest. The fact that such a field has such an impact on todays and the futures society pretty much sold me. So I have spent a lot of time learning the field and finding my place in the massive frame-work of the industry. Actually, this website is the first accomplishment of mine in the field. I will continue my passion in this market by not only website designing, but I would also like my artistic side to surface and expand as well by designing logos, back drops, characters, and more. I not only want my websites functional, but I want them visually stimulating as well. I want to leave my imprint.

WHO IS SHE

Natalia Sturza

By day, I test software as an automation developer; by night, I test boundaries of normality by exploring myself and the wide wild world around me. I have high standards when it comes to quality analysis and work ethics, as well as to myself (if not higher). In my never-ending pursuit for excellence, I never stop learning, exploring, experiencing. Challenge is my fuel, motivation is my road, positive results of my work is my reward. I have lived in several countries, finished several highest degrees and worked in various positions from a director of development in a rare disease non-profit foundation, to a current job of a software automation developer in a large bank. Software is my passion. It is like a living and breathing organism, always changing, always evolving, never the same. Keeping up with the IT industry pushes me to always be on my tippy toes. I do freelancing on uTest, UpWork and Testilo to stay current with my competition. Aside of testing software, I enjoy testing my physical abilities by working out daily, as the saying goes, 'Mens sana in corpore sano', a healthy mind in a healthy body. I also test my caffeine tolerance by being an avid coffee drinker. I am always open to exploring all things new and all things unexperienced. I am intrigued by traveling, art, armchair philosophy, weird facts, podcasts, fabulous food, and even better conversations. Always seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it, and to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me.
