Why invest in the real estate market. Lets look at some main focus points

  • Cash Flow: Can be the net gain from a rental properly after all general mortgage expenses are deducted. Which is received every month, or you can break even and have that rental income cover your mortgage.
  • Property Depreciation: The government will allow owners a deduction from their taxes of the property over it's life span.
  • Tax Deductions and Benefits: The owner can usually claim the interest gained from the monthly mortgage payment as an additional tax deduction. Repairs or improvements on the property can be written off as a tax deductions as well. As a real estate investor, keeping your property a float is a business, thus there are many business expenses that can be written off as well from office supplies used in relation to the property, gas used to driving to properties or investment related trips, etc. There are many write-offs one can gain from. Make sure to higher a great CPA.
  • Leverage: As some lenders will allow a down payment as low as 3.5% (FHA Loan) up to 20% (conventional load), one could really spread the money around. Let's say you have $100,000.00 is assets to spend, you may be able to purchase 2 or 3 properties instead of paying for 1 property in full, thus may result in a large return from all 3 versus the 1.
  • Amortization: Basically paying down loans. Which will free up more capital to increase the amount you can leverage towards another potential property. Yes, that's right, once you have 20%+ paid off on a property, you can free up some of that money and put towards a down payment on another property.

So above are a few examples why real estate is a sure way to goes when is comes to putting your cash towards a safe stable investment.

Why invest in coffee parcels? Lets bring perspective. Coffee is the 2nd most traded commodity in the world as the runner-up to the 1st most traded commodity in the world, oil. Even one with half a brain cell realizes there may be something to investments in coffee parcels.

Coffee parcels can run as low as $15,000.00 which provides you a turn-key investment. Basically you provide the capital, and the farms will provide the labor, production and distribution. You gain a small percent in the first coming years, but after several years, you can gain as high as 49% returns from your parcel. The other benefit is this investment is outside of state. So U.S. laws and regulations have no effect on your land, yes it is your land. Uncle Sam can't tough this.

Why no invest in a constant growing investment where you receive a continuous return every year, while providing a sustainable life for the local workers whom work your land. Yes, more than a quarter of the gains from your parcel will go back to the people who work your parcel. I don't know about you, but gaining money and helping lives, it's a win win
